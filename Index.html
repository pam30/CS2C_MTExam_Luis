Discover Ilocos Norte

Explore scenic churches, wind farms and white limestone formations. This mini-site highlights three must-see tourist spots in Ilocos.

Featured Tourist Spots & Adventures

Paoay Church (St. Augustine Church)

Paoay Church is an iconic UNESCO heritage baroque church known for its massive buttresses and historic architecture. Visitors are often amazed by the dramatic façade and well preserved interior, perfect for photography and history walks. Nearby cafes and local vendors offer Ilocano specialties after a reflective tour of the church grounds.

#PaoayChurch #IlocosHeritage #BaroqueArchitecture #HistoryWalk #IlocosTravel

Bangui Windmills

The Bangui Wind Farm features long rows of giant wind turbines standing in the sea along the shore — a dramatic, photogenic landmark. It’s a great spot for sunset photography while enjoying the cool coastal breeze and wide ocean views. Small stalls nearby sell snacks and souvenirs; many visitors combine this with a day trip along the coastal highway.

#BanguiWindmills #RenewableEnergy #IlocosCoast #SunsetSpot #PhotoHotspot

Kapurpurawan Rock Formation

Kapurpurawan is a striking white limestone formation sculpted by wind and waves into fantastical shapes along the coast. The bright rocks against the deep blue sea make for dramatic views and unique photo opportunities. Walk the coastal path and be prepared for some short climbs — wear sturdy shoes and bring water.
